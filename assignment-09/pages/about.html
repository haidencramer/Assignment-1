We are here to help you snowboard like a pro!

This organization was created in order to help individuals learn how to prepare for the snowboarding season, and feel confident when they are ready to go to the hill! Snowboarding is a very fun but, very demanding task that requires lots of focus and the right equipment to stay warm/safe. We want people all over the world to experience what riding a snowboard has to offer.


Missoula, Montana (a.k.a shredding paradise):

Our organization is located in the heart of a city in Montana named Missoula. We were founded in 1990 back when snowboarding was finally starting to become normalized across mainy different ski resorts. Volunteers in our company believe that Missoula offers the best close by terrain as well as many good street spots for the park rats out there!
